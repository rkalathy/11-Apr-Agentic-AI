# PRD Review Report for Website Chat Thing

## Executive Summary
The PRD for the Website Chat Thing requires significant revisions before approval. The overall verdict is that the document is currently **Not Ready** for implementation due to critical ambiguities and missing details across technical, UX, and business strategy aspects. 

### Top 3 Blockers
1. **Requirement Ambiguity:** The role of the admin in the chat feature is unclear, leading to potential misalignment in expectations.
2. **Missing Technical Details:** Lack of defined APIs, data models, and performance targets hampers development clarity.
3. **Unvalidated Business Assumptions:** The absence of market research and clear success metrics limits the understanding of the business opportunity.

---

## Recommended Next Steps Before PRD Approval

3. **Conduct Market Research:** Validate the business opportunity and target market through research.
4. **Revise User Stories:** Expand user stories and acceptance criteria to cover all user roles and scenarios.
5. **Address Security and Scalability:** Develop a comprehensive security plan and scalability strategy.
6. **Validate Assumptions:** Gather user feedback to confirm assumptions regarding chat features and user preferences.

This report should serve as a guide for the product team to address the identified issues and improve the PRD for the Website Chat Thing. Immediate action on the prioritized items is essential for moving forward effectively.
