EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT POLICY

1. Eligible Expenses
   - Travel (flights, trains, cabs for official purposes)
   - Hotel accommodation during official travel
   - Meals during official travel (up to Rs 1,500 per day)
   - Client entertainment (with prior approval)
   - Training and certification fees (with manager approval)
   - Books and learning materials (up to Rs 5,000 per year)

2. Expense Limits
   - Domestic travel: Economy class for flights under 4 hours, business class for longer flights (VP and above).
   - Hotel: Up to Rs 5,000 per night for metros, Rs 3,500 per night for other cities.
   - Local conveyance: Actuals with receipts; cab rides via company-approved apps only.

3. Submission Process
   - All expenses must be submitted within 15 days of incurring the expense.
   - Receipts are mandatory for all expenses above Rs 500.
   - Expenses must be submitted through the HR portal.
   - Manager approval is required for all claims.

4. Reimbursement Timeline
   - Approved expenses are reimbursed within 7 working days.
   - Reimbursement is credited to the employee's salary account.

5. Non-Reimbursable Expenses
   - Personal travel or entertainment
   - Alcohol (unless part of approved client entertainment)
   - Fines or penalties
   - Personal phone bills (company provides a phone allowance separately)

6. Travel Advance
   - Employees can request a travel advance up to Rs 20,000 for domestic travel.
   - The advance must be settled within 7 days of return.
