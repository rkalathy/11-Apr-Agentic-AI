LEAVE POLICY

1. Annual Leave (Earned Leave)
   - All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
   - Leave is credited at the rate of 1.67 days per month.
   - Unused leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 10 days to the next year.
   - Leave beyond 30 days will lapse at the end of the calendar year.
   - Annual leave must be approved by the reporting manager at least 3 days in advance.

2. Sick Leave
   - Employees are entitled to 10 days of sick leave per year.
   - Sick leave of more than 2 consecutive days requires a medical certificate.
   - Sick leave cannot be carried forward or encashed.

3. Casual Leave
   - Employees are entitled to 7 days of casual leave per year.
   - Casual leave cannot be taken for more than 3 consecutive days.
   - Prior approval is required except in emergencies.

4. Maternity Leave
   - Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks (182 days) of paid maternity leave.
   - This applies to employees who have worked for at least 80 days in the 12 months preceding the expected delivery date.
   - Maternity leave can be taken up to 8 weeks before the expected delivery date.

5. Paternity Leave
   - Male employees are entitled to 10 days of paid paternity leave.
   - Must be availed within 30 days of the child's birth.

6. Compensatory Off
   - Employees who work on holidays or weekends are entitled to compensatory off.
   - Compensatory off must be availed within 30 days of the extra working day.
   - Prior approval from the manager is required.

7. Loss of Pay (LOP)
   - If an employee exhausts all leave balances, further absence will be treated as Loss of Pay.
   - LOP will be deducted from the monthly salary on a pro-rata basis.
