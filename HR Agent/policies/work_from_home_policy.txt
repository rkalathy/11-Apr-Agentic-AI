WORK FROM HOME POLICY

1. Eligibility
   - All full-time employees who have completed their probation period (6 months) are eligible for work from home.
   - Interns and contract employees are not eligible unless approved by the department head.

2. Work From Home Days
   - Employees can work from home up to 2 days per week (Tuesday and Thursday are preferred WFH days).
   - The remaining 3 days must be spent in the office.
   - Employees must inform their manager at least 1 day in advance.

3. Core Working Hours
   - Employees must be available during core hours: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM IST.
   - Flexible start time: between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM.
   - Total working hours: 8 hours per day.

4. Equipment and Setup
   - The company provides a laptop for all employees.
   - Employees are responsible for maintaining a stable internet connection (minimum 10 Mbps).
   - A one-time WFH setup allowance of Rs 5,000 is provided.

5. Communication
   - Employees must be reachable via Slack and email during working hours.
   - Video must be turned on during team meetings.
   - Daily standup attendance is mandatory.

6. Exceptions
   - During critical project phases, the manager may require full in-office attendance.
   - Employees with performance concerns may have WFH privileges revoked.
