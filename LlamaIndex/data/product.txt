NimbusTech Product Overview

The flagship product is Nimbus Observe, a unified platform for traces,
metrics, and logs. It supports OpenTelemetry natively and ingests data over
OTLP/gRPC and OTLP/HTTP. Default data retention is 30 days on the Pro plan
and 13 months on the Enterprise plan.

Pricing tiers (per month, billed annually):
- Free: up to 5 GB ingest, 7-day retention, community support.
- Pro: $89 per host, 30-day retention, email support.
- Enterprise: custom pricing, 13-month retention, 24/7 SLA, dedicated CSM.

Nimbus Observe ships SDKs for Go, Python, Java, Node.js, and Rust. The
Python SDK is the most popular, accounting for roughly 41 percent of
ingested spans across all customers.

Integrations available out-of-the-box include AWS CloudWatch, GCP
Operations Suite, Kubernetes, Kafka, and Postgres. A Slack integration for
alerts ships with all paid plans.

Known limitations as of v4.2 (March 2026):
- No support for AWS GovCloud regions.
- Trace sampling is configurable per service but not per endpoint.
- The free tier does not support SSO.
