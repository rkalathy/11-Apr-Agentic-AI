NimbusTech Inc.

NimbusTech was founded in 2019 in Berlin by Lena Holtz and Dario Marini. The
company builds developer tools for cloud-native observability. Headquarters
are in Berlin with engineering hubs in Lisbon and Bangalore.

As of Q1 2026 the company has 187 employees, of which 124 are engineers.
NimbusTech raised a Series B of $42 million in October 2025 led by
Northwind Ventures, with participation from Atlas Capital and existing
investors. Total funding to date stands at $63 million.

The leadership team:
- CEO: Lena Holtz
- CTO: Dario Marini
- VP Engineering: Priya Iyer
- VP Sales: Marcus Olufsen

NimbusTech is bootstrapped on profitability for paid plans and runs a
generous open-source tier under the Apache 2.0 license.
